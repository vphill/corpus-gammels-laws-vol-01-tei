The constitutional Governor of the State of Coahuila and Texas, to all
the inhabitants thereof: Be it known, that the congress of said state has
decreed as follows:
DECEEE No. 324.
The Congress of the State of Coahuila. and Texas has thought proper
to decree:
The law of the 11th of January 1834, authorizing the executive for
the pursuit and extermination of robbers is hereby repealed.
For its fulfilment, the Governor of the State shall cause it to be
printed, published, and circulated.
