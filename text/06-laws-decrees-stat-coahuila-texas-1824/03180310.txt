DECEEE No. 202.
The Congress of the State of Coahuila and Texas has thought proper
to decree:
Art. 1. The five per cent, consumption duty on foreign effects shall
be increased one per cent., for the municipal funds of the state.
Art. 2. The revenue agents shall take care to make this collection,
and deliver to the respective Ayuntamientos monthly, the amount col-
lected.
Art. 3. This decree shall go into effect in eight days from the pub-
lication thereof.
For its fulfilment, the Governor of the State shall cause it to be
printed, published, and circulated.
Given in the city of Leona Vicario on the 29th September, 1832.
MANUEL MUZQUIZ, D. President.
JOSE CAYETANO EAMOS, D. S.
FRANCISCO S. de AEEEOLA, D. S.
