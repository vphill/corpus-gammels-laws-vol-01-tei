The Governor of the State of Coahuila and Texas, to all the inhab-
itants thereof: Be it known, that the congress of said state has decreed ■
as follows:
DECREE No. 185.
Three years additional term is hereby granted to John Cameron to
fulfill the colonization contracts which he ratified with the executive
of the state, on the 21st of May 1827, and 19th of September, 1828.
For its fulfilment, the Governor of the State shall cause it to be
printed, published, and circulated.
Given in the city of Leona Vicario on the 3d of April, 1832.
JOSE JESUS GRANDE, D. President.
MANUEL HERNANDEZ, D. S.
MANUEL MUZQUIZ, D. S.
Wherefore I command it to be printed, published, circulated and duly
fulfilled.
