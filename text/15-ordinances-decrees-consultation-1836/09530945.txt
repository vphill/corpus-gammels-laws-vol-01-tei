cree for advancing the sum of fifteen hundred dollars for the
benefit of the Volunteer Army of the People under the com-
mand of General S. F. Austin, and other purposes,” be, and
is hereby, repealed; and that the said-Thomas Gay be directed
to return to the Treasurer, the money he may have received,
by virtue of said Ordinance, which is so repealed.
