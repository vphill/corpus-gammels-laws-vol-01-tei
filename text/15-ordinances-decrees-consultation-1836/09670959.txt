Sec. 4. Be it further ordained and decreed, &c., That
the aforesaid Contractor, William Pettus, give bond and se-
curity in the amount of two thousand dollars for the faithful
performance of his duty as Contractor; and that the said bond
be taken in the name of the Provisional Governor, and his
successor in office.
Sec. 5. Be it further ordained and decreed, &c., That
the said Contractor, William Pettus be, and is hereby author-
ized to appoint one or more deputies, should it become neces-
sary; and that the said Contractor be held responsible for all
the acts of his deputy or deputies, while in the discharge of
their duties or duty as Contractor or Contractors.
Passed at San Felipe de Austin, Dec. 5, 1835.
