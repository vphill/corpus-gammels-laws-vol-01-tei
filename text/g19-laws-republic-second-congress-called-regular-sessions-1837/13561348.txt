SAM. HOUSTON.
JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of W. S. Hendrick.
Resolved by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas in congress assembled, That the sum of one hun-
dred and sixty-two dollars be allowed to W. S. Hendrick, for ex-
tra services rendered the first congress as door-keeper, and that
this resolution be a sufficient voucher to the auditor of public ac-
counts to allow and audit the same.
JOSEHH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Oct. 25, 1837.
