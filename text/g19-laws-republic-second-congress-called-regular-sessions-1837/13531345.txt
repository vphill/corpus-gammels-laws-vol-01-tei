JOINT RESOLUTION
To. suspend the operation of the Land Office until the further
tion of congress
Resolved by the senate and house of representatives of the
ac-
re-
public of Texas in congress assembled. That so much of an act
entitled “an act supplementary to an act establishing a general land
office for the republic of Texas,” passed 26th December, 1836, as
provided for the opening of the land office on the first day of
October, 1837, and for the appointment of officer under said law,
be, and the same is hereby suspended until the further action of
congress.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Sept. 30, 1837.
