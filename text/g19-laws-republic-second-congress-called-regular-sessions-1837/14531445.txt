SAM. HOUSTON.
AN ACT
Declaring certain children Legitimate, therein named.
Sec. 1. Be it enacted, by the senate and house of representa-
tives of the republic of Texas, in congress assembled, That Elijah
Sterling Black Robertson, son of Sterling C. Robertson and Fanny
King, and James Macklin Robertson, son of Sterling C. Robertson
and Rachael Smith, be, and are hereby declared legitimate chil-
dren, and capable in law of inheriting their parents’ property, in
the same manner as if they had been born in lawful wedlock.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
