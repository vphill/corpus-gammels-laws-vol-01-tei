JOINT RESOLUTION,
For the relief of Maria Antonio de La Garza.
Resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas, in congress assembled, That the first auditor is
hereby authorized to audit the claim of Maria Antonio de La Garza,
for cattle, as per certificates of Sylvanus Hatch, John J. Linn, and
general T. J. Rusk, for sixty head of cattle,'at ten dollars per head,
six hundred dollars in military scrip.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
MIRABEAU B. LAMAR,
President of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
SAM. HOUSTON.
