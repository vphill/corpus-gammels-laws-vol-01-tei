SAM. HOUSTON.
JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of M. A. Beremendi.
Resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas in congress assembled, That Marcus A. Bere-
mendi, a native of Texas, a minor, of about twenty years of age,
be, and he is hereby authorized and empowered with the consent
of his guardian to take possession of his patrimonial estate, and
his acts in all respects shall be as legal and valid as though he were
of lawful age; Provided, that the passage of this resolution shall not
operate to make or cause a confirmation of his titles.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro- tem. of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
