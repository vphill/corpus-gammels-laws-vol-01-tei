SAM. HOUSTON.
AN ACT
To authorize the President to appoint a' Commissioner to run the
Boundary line between the United States of America, and
Republic of Texas.
Be it enacted by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas, in Congress assembled, That the president be and
he is hereby authorized and empowered to appoint a commissioner,
by and with the advice and consent of the senate, to unite with
such commissioner as may be appointed by the United States of
America, to run and mark the boundary line between the two gov-
ernments, from latitude second degree north on the Sabine river,
to the Rio Roxo or the Red River, agreeable to the treaty made
and entered into, between the said United States and Spain, in the
year one thousand eight hundred and nineteen.
