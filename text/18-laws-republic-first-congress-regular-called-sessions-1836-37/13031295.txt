SAM. HOUSTON.
A JOINT RESOLUTION
Respecting Mail Routes.
Resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas, in congress assembled. That the post master gen-
eral of this republic be authorized to extend the mail routes by way
of Ballou’s and Gaines’ ferries, so as to communicate with the
■ United States mail, and that he be authorized to pay for any ser-
vices which may have been already rendered by any person to
affect that object.
B. T. ARCHER,
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
JESSE GRIMES,
President pro tem. of the Senate.
Approved, June 5, 1837.
