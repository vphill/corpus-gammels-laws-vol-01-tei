AN ACT
Concerning fees of Sheriffs, Constables and Solicitors.
Be it enacted, by the Senate and House of Representatives of
the republic of Texas, in Congress assembled, That in addition to
the fees already allowed to sheriffs and constables, they shall be
allowed to charge and receive five cents for every mile they must
necessarily travel, in going and returning on business of their
office, and to solicitors shall be allowed for all convictions under
the gambling act. the same fees as are allowed in cases of felony.
