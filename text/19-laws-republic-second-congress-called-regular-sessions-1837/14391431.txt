SAM. HOUSTON.
JOINT RESOLUTION,
Requiring the Auditor to settle with Mr. Thomas Brennan.
Resolved by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas in congress assembled, That the auditor be re-
quired to audit and settle with Mr. Thomas Brennan, purser of the
schooner Independence, the claims of the officers and crew of that
vessel.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
