JOINT RESOLUTION
Tor the relief of Dugald Brown.
Resolved by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas, in congress assembled, That the auditor be, and
he is hereby directed to audit the claims of Dugald Brown, for the
sum of seven hundred and seventy dollars.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the Senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
