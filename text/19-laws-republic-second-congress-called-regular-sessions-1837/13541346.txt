JOINT RESOLUTION
Making appropriation of moneys for the use of the Hospital.
Be it resolved by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas in Congress assembled, That the sum of one
thousand dollars be, and the same is hereby appropriated out of
any moneys in the Treasury or in the hands of the officers of the
custom-houses, to be expended under the direction of the secre-
tary of war, to provide for the comforts of the sick soldiers, who
.now are, or hereafter may be, in the hospital at this place.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Oct. 15, 1837.
SAM. HOUSTON.
