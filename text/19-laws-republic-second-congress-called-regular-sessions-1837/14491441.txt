JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of C. C. Dewitt.
Be it resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas, in congress assembled, That the auditor is here-
by authorized to audit the claims of Christopher C. Dewitt, for ser-
vices rendered by him in the fall campaign, as per documents re-
ferred to the committee on claims and accounts, being for eighty
seven dollars.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
MIRABEAU B. LAMAR,
President'of the senate.
