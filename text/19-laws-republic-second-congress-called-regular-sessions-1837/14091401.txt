JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of Anson Jones.
Resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas, in congress assembled, That the first auditor be re-
quired to audit the accounts of Dr. Anson Jones, for the sum of
fourteen hundred and sixty-five dollars thirty-three and one-third
cents, for his sendees as apothecary general, from the 6th of Octo-
ber 1836, to the 10th May, 1837; also two hundred and ten dollars
expended by him for the rent of a store house from 1st July 1836,
to the 1st day of February, 1837.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
MIRABEAU B. LAMAR,
President of the senate.
Approved. Dec. 14, 1837.
SAM. HOUSTON.
