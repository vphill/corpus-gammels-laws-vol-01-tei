JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of John A. Wharton.
Be it resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas, in congress assembled, That the auditor be in-
structed to pay to John A. Wharton, or his representatives five hun-
dred dollars, in the promissory notes of this government, for ser-
vices and expenses incurred by him in going to and from Mata-
moras.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 21, 1837.
SAM. HOUSTON.
