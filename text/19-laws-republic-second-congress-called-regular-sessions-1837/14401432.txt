JOINT RESOLUTION
Altering the name of the county and town of Mina.
Eesolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the re-
public of Texas in congress assembled, That the name of the county
and town of Mina be changed and altered to that of Bastrop, and
shall be after the passage of this resolution known and called the
county of Bastrop, and town of Bastrop.
JOSEPH EOWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
S. H. EVEEITT,
President pro tern, of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
