JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of Widow Kitty McCoy.
Be it resolved, by the senate and house of representatives of the
republic of Texas, in congress assembled. That the first auditor
is authorized to audit the claim of widow Kitty McCoy as per vouch-
ers of Byrd Lockhart and colonel William H. Patterson for beef
and corn, valued at three hundred and seventy-eight dollars in
military scrip.
JOSEPH ROWE,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
. S. H. EVERITT,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, Dec. 18, 1837.
SAM. HOUSTON.
