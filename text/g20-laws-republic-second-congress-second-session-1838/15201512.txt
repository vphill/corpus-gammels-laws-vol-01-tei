SAM. HOUSTON.
AN ACT
To Amend the Direct Tax Law.
Sec. 1. Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representa-
tives of the Republic of Texas in Congress assembled, That.
it shall be the duty of the assessors, so soon as they may be noti-
fied of their appointment, to advertise in at least three of the
most public places within each captain’s beat, giving at least
twenty days notice to all persons living within the bounds of
the same, that he will attend at the mustering grounds for said
beats on a particular day or days, for the purpose of receiving
a list of taxable property, at which time and place he shall at-
tend, and remain a sufficient length of time to enable all persons
who may attend to give in their several lists.
