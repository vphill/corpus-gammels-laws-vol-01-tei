EESOLUTION,
For repairing the President’s House, and purchasing Furniture.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of
the Republic of Texas in Congress assembled, That the sum of
three thousand dollars of the Promissory Notes of this Gov-
ernment be, and the same is hereby appropriated to complete
and repairs and purchase furniture for the President’s House;
and that the said sum be expended under the direction of his Ex-
cellency.
JOSEPH EOWE,
Speaker of the House of Representatives.
MIEABEAU B. LAMAE,
President of the Senate.
Approved, May 11, 1838.
