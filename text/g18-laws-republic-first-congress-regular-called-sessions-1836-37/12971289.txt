SAM. HOUSTON.
JOINT RESOLUTION
For the relief of Capt. John M. Allen.
Be it enacted, by the Senate and House of Representatives of
the republic of Texas, in Congress assembled, That the auditor be
instructed to settle the accounts of Capt. John M. Allen, on the
principles of equity and justice.
B. T. ARCHER,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
JESSE GRIMES,
President pro tem. of the senate.
Approved, May 29, 1837.
