JOINT RESOLUTION
Requiring the President to appoint a Secretary of Legation to the
Embassy to. England.
Be it resolved, by the Senate and House of Representatives of
the Republic of Texas, in Congress assembled, That the president
he, and is hereby requested to appoint, by and with the advice and
consent of the senate, a secretary of legation to the embassy to
England, which secretary shall receive three thousand dollars per
annum, to be paid out of any money in the treasury not otherwise
appropriated.
