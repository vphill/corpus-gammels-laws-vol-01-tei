JOINT RESOLUTION
Requiring the Auditor of public accounts to audit the accounts
of J. Bryant and S. Francois.
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Re-
public of Texas, in congress assembled, That the auditor of public
accounts is hereby authorized and required to audit the accounts
of John Bryant for three hundred and sixty-four dollars; and also
to audit the account of Sebastian Francois for the sum of two hun-
dred and fifty-nine dollars: and further, that said Bryant and
Sebastian are entitled to twelve hundred and eighty acres land
each, as bounty land for services rendered this republic as soldiers.
B. T. ARCHER,
Speaker of the house of representatives.
JESSE GRIMES,
President pro tem. of the Senate.
'Approved, June 9, 1837.
