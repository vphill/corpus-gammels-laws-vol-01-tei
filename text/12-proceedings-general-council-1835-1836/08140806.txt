Council Hall, San Felipe de Austin, )
February 8th, 1836. f
nine o’clock, a. M.
The Council met pursuant to adjournment.
Mr. J. S. Lane presented his certificate of election as a member from
Sabine, took the oath prescribed by the-organic law, and was admitted
to a seat.
Francis W. Jackson was appointed a clerk to assist in transcribing,
&c., and was sworn.
Mr. Tucker, from Shelby, asked and obtained leave of absence to re-
turn home.
Mr. Lane, was on motion, added to the committee on finance.
On motion, the Council adjourned till nine o’clock to-morrow morn-
ing.
